About Me

I am an Instructional Designer specializing in leveraging Generative AI to create accessible, high-impact learning materials at scale. I design, prototype, and ship training for fast-moving teams that need quality, speed, and consistency.

My philosophy is built on Universal Design for Learning (UDL)—ensuring that efficiency never comes at the cost of accessibility. I build AI-augmented workflows that cut development time by 60–75% while maintaining strong learning outcomes and a polished learner experience.
